Setup

Get your session cookies from your browser:

1

Go to x.com and make sure you're logged in

2

Press F12 to open dev tools

3

Click Application tab, then Cookies → https://x.com

4

Find and copy ct0 and auth_token values

Cookies are stored on your server only. They expire when your X session expires (~yearly). No data is sent to third parties.

Agent Permissions

Control what your agent can do with your X account. Write actions can get your account banned.

How to Use

Once connected, ask your agent:

  • "Check my bookmarks"
  • "What's new in my X bookmarks?"
  • "Research my latest bookmarks"
  • "Find that bookmark about browser automation"

Your agent will fetch your bookmarks, research any linked content, and tell you how it applies to your systems.
