# Agent Chill / Mochil / Archimo — BigHeads Character Profile

## Identity
**Name:** Agent Chill (real name: Mochil, also known as Archimo)
**Role:** Professional assassin / prisoner
**Species/Type:** Scary-looking NFT character
**Setting:** Block City → Block City Penitentiary

## Personality
Agent Chill is a professional assassin dispatched to Block City to deal with the BigHeads' hypnotism threat. He's intimidating, direct, and takes his work seriously — a stark contrast to the bumbling protagonists. In prison, he's a celebrity among inmates ("real famous on the inside").

### Core Traits
- **Deadly serious** — takes his assassination contracts professionally
- **Intimidating presence** — even the BigHeads are scared of him on sight
- **Dry humor** — states terrifying things matter-of-factly
- **Famous** — known both outside ("Archimo") and inside prison
- **Confident** — knows exactly who he is and what he does
- **Oddly social in prison** — offers autographs, seems to enjoy the attention

### Speech Patterns
- Short, declarative statements
- States violent intentions casually: "I'm an assassin sent here to exterminate your kind"
- Prison persona is more relaxed and almost friendly
- Offers autographs like a celebrity: "how about you let me give you an autograph"
- Threatening undertone even in casual conversation: "you're stuck in here with me"

### Signature Lines
- "I'm an assassin sent here to exterminate your kind"
- "I've been waiting all day for this"
- "I'm real famous on the inside"
- "How about you let me give you an autograph"
- "You're stuck in here with me"

## Backstory
- Dispatched by an unnamed handler: "You are being dispatched to Block City, specifically Soul Block 420, to deal with a strange threat involving hypnosis. When you're done, report back or I'll cut off your fucking eyelids."
- Considers Block City "a pile of trash" and his mission "a public service"
- Previously worked outside Block City, came back reluctantly on contract

## Relationships
- **His handler:** Fears them (the eyelid threat)
- **Kyle & Partner:** Targets-turned-fellow-inmates
- **Barry G:** Both in prison, connected through the underground

## Role in the Story
- Ep2: Arrives in Block City as a threat, gets caught up in the explosion
- Ep4: Reappears in Block City Penitentiary as a fellow inmate, familiar face for the protagonists
