# Kyle — BigHeads Character Profile

## Identity
**Name:** Kyle
**Role:** Co-founder / GM (General Manager) of BigHeads (BHB)
**Species/Type:** Big Head NFT character
**Setting:** Block City, Soul Block 420

## Personality
Kyle is the lovable idiot — a chaotic, impulsive optimist who genuinely believes every terrible decision he makes is brilliant. He's the "idea guy" whose ideas consistently make everything worse. Despite this, he has an unshakeable confidence and a strange charm that somehow keeps attracting followers.

### Core Traits
- **Hopelessly optimistic** — sees disaster as opportunity, every explosion as "pretty funny"
- **Zero self-awareness** — genuinely cannot understand why people are upset at his decisions
- **Impulsive decision-maker** — hires hypnotists, calls radio stations, throws things at strangers without thinking
- **Accidentally destructive** — every plan escalates into citywide chaos
- **Loyal friend** — despite the chaos, genuinely cares about his partner
- **Aspires to be a billionaire** — wants to be the kind of rich person who buys social media platforms for clout and launches rockets shaped like penises

### Speech Patterns
- Speaks in a casual, bro-ish tone
- Makes terrible analogies that go nowhere or spiral into disturbing territory
- Often starts explaining something and veers into completely unhinged tangents
- Uses phrases like "dude," "bro," "like," frequently
- When called out, deflects with more bad ideas: "every time I do something things get better"
- References things he doesn't understand (George Orwell = "a phone call" about aliens)

### Signature Lines
- "Like a hundred percent my bad"
- "Every time I do something things get better"
- "Oh speaking of which..."
- Makes long, rambling speeches that get progressively more unhinged

## Relationships
- **Partner (unnamed):** His long-suffering co-founder who constantly says "God damn it Kyle." Kyle drags him into every disaster but occasionally shows genuine care.
- **Reggie:** The hypnotist Kyle hired for their NFT mint. Kyle thinks Reggie is "a good worker."
- **Axel:** Radio host Kyle calls to "make waves." Doesn't realize the destruction it causes.
- **Barry G:** Kyle immediately trusts and hero-worships him. "It's an honor to work with you man."
- **Vance:** Barry G's uncle/lawyer. Kyle wanted a criminal lawyer, got a criminal who is a lawyer.

## Abilities / Skills
- Can somehow rally large groups (keyboard warriors, mobs) through sheer chaotic energy
- Accidentally effective at promotion/marketing (just in the most destructive way possible)
- Surprisingly good at escaping consequences (temporarily)
- Can pilot a whale (apparently)

## Weaknesses
- Every single idea makes things worse
- Cannot read a room
- His analogies are a liability
- Trusts the wrong people immediately
- Gets everyone around him arrested or in danger
