# Axel — BigHeads Character Profile

## Identity
**Name:** Axel
**Role:** Radio host / media personality
**Species/Type:** Appears to be a lizard character (referenced "lizard neck")
**Setting:** Block City radio station

## Personality
Axel is an aggressive, hyper-masculine radio host who runs Block City's airwaves. He's the crypto-bro version of a shock jock — bullying callers, dismissing complaints, and projecting an "alpha" persona that's equal parts entertaining and insufferable. He operates from his "kingdom" above the city.

### Core Traits
- **Aggressively alpha** — dominates every conversation, insults anyone who shows weakness
- **Narcissistic** — genuinely believes he's above everyone
- **Crypto-bro energy** — talks in terms of "alpha space" vs "beta shit"
- **Zero accountability** — when callers lose money on his advice, it's "NFA bro"
- **Entertaining despite himself** — his over-the-top personality draws listeners
- **Dismissive** — cuts off callers mid-sentence, insults their intelligence

### Speech Patterns
- Rapid-fire, aggressive delivery
- Crypto/trading slang: "NFA," "alpha space," "beta shit," "smooth brains"
- Insults as punctuation — nearly every response includes a putdown
- Self-aggrandizing: "only for big boys," "you don't qualify today sir"
- Dismissive catchphrases when he's done with someone: "next," "get butt punched"
- Has a musical intro/theme: "big bad cause I'm plugged in the network, whole team winning, check a chain for the net worth"

### Signature Lines
- "You're gonna stay a listener if you don't — this is alpha space"
- "All this is NFA, not financial advice, get butt punched"
- "You must be new man"
- "These fucking smooth brains"
- "It's tucked away in my pants up here, only for big boys, so you don't qualify today sir"

## Relationships
- **Kyle:** Unknowingly gives Kyle a platform that causes citywide destruction
- **Callers:** Treats them as content fodder and punching bags
- **His audience:** Rules over them from his "kingdom"

## Role in the Story
His radio show becomes the catalyst for the Keyboard Warriors uprising in Ep2. Kyle calls in, Axel's aggressive dismissal of another caller sparks outrage, and the resulting chaos leads to an explosion that rocks Block City.

## Abilities
- Commands a large audience through sheer personality
- Can unknowingly incite riots
- Immune to criticism (or just doesn't process it)
