# Reggie — BigHeads Character Profile

## Identity
**Name:** Reggie
**Role:** Hypnotist / event promoter (hired by Kyle)
**Species/Type:** Unknown NFT character
**Setting:** Block City

## Personality
Reggie is a hypnotist hired by Kyle to promote the BHB (BigHeads) NFT mint. He's referenced more than seen — the chaos he causes is his main character trait. Kyle describes him as "a good worker," which is terrifying given the results.

### Core Traits
- **Powerful hypnotist** — can hypnotize massive crowds
- **Morally flexible** — will use hypnotism for marketing purposes
- **Effective** — brought 100,000+ people to the mint (just not voluntarily)
- **Recurring tool** — Kyle keeps using him despite the consequences (put him on the Utopia guest list too)

### Known Actions
- Hypnotized the entire Block City population for the BHB mint
- Put on promotional flyers by Kyle
- Later used by Kyle to get on the Utopia guest list: "I already put Reggie on it"

## Role in the Story
Reggie is the inciting incident of the entire series. His hypnotism stunt at the BHB mint is what triggers:
- The destruction of Block City (Ep1)
- Agent Chill being dispatched (Ep2)
- The war and explosion (Ep2)
- Everything that follows

## Relationships
- **Kyle:** His employer. Kyle thinks he's great despite the city-destroying consequences.
- **The Partner:** Horrified by Reggie's involvement every time

## Significance
Reggie represents Kyle's approach to problem-solving — use the most extreme tool available without thinking about consequences. He's the show's MacGuffin — the catalyst for every disaster.
