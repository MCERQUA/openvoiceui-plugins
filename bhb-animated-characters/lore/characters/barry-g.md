# Barry G — BigHeads Character Profile

## Identity
**Name:** Barry G (also referred to as "Very G")
**Role:** Infamous garage sale con artist / prison fixer
**Species/Type:** NFT character
**Setting:** Utopia → Block City Penitentiary

## Personality
Barry G is a smooth-talking criminal legend who operates as a connector and fixer. He's respected (or at least feared) by everyone — referred to as "a legend" and "infamous." He knows everyone, has a plan for everything, and always seems one step ahead. He's the classic "guy who knows a guy" character.

### Core Traits
- **Smooth operator** — always calm, always has a plan
- **Connected** — knows everyone from con artists to prison bosses
- **Legendary reputation** — even strangers recognize and respect him
- **Mysterious** — never fully reveals his cards
- **Mentor-like** — takes the BigHeads under his wing in prison
- **Confident** — "glad you finally came to your senses"

### Speech Patterns
- Calm, measured delivery — never panicked
- Speaks like someone who's seen it all
- Reassuring but vague: "don't worry, I got a plan"
- Knows when to let things play out: "well I guess we'll just have to see how it goes"
- Uses "boys" as an address: "what are your boys saying," "glad you finally came to your senses"

### Signature Lines
- "You boys want to get out of here? Stick with me, I got a plan"
- "Glad you finally came to your senses"
- "You're not going to be learning from me — you're gonna be learning from him"

## Relationships
- **Vance (uncle):** His uncle is a lawyer — a terrible one, but family
- **Kyle:** Kyle hero-worships him immediately
- **The Partner:** More skeptical of Barry G's intentions
- **"The Big Boss":** Connected to the prison's top figure (Tate parody)

## Role in the Story
- Ep3: Appears during the arrest in Utopia, offers to help them escape
- Ep4: Reconnects with them in prison, introduces them to the big boss
- Serves as the bridge character between the outside world and the prison arc

## Backstory
- "Infamous garage sale con artist" — his specific criminal specialty
- Has been in and out of the system
- Connected enough to arrange meetings with the prison's power structure
