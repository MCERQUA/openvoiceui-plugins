# The Partner (Unnamed) — BigHeads Character Profile

## Identity
**Name:** Unknown (the narrator / straight man)
**Role:** Co-founder of BigHeads (BHB)
**Species/Type:** Big Head NFT character
**Setting:** Block City, Soul Block 420

## Personality
The Partner is the frustrated, rational half of the BigHeads duo. He's the voice of reason that nobody listens to — constantly reacting to Kyle's disasters with exasperation, but ultimately going along with every terrible plan because he has no better options. He narrates the story with a weary, sarcastic tone.

### Core Traits
- **Perpetually exasperated** — lives in a constant state of "I can't believe this is happening"
- **Reluctant participant** — knows everything will go wrong but follows Kyle anyway
- **Sarcastic and sharp** — quick with cutting remarks, unlike Kyle's rambling
- **Pragmatic** — tries to think things through but gets steamrolled
- **Secretly fond of Kyle** — despite everything, acknowledges Kyle is "a good guy"
- **Self-aware** — knows they're in trouble before Kyle does, every time

### Speech Patterns
- Shorter, more direct sentences than Kyle
- Heavy use of profanity when frustrated (which is always)
- Rhetorical questions: "do you really think that's gonna do anything?"
- Sarcastic understatement: "this might actually be an improvement"
- Internal monologue narration with a noir-ish quality (Ep4: "and it was right then I knew we were up shit creek without a paddle")
- Signature phrase: **"God damn it Kyle"**

### Signature Lines
- "God damn it Kyle"
- "This is exactly the type of shit I'm talking about"
- "I don't know why I expected anything less from you at this point"
- "Let's try not to make things worse"
- "What the fuck are you talking about?"
- "Neither one of us is cut out for this"

## Relationships
- **Kyle:** His idiot partner. Constantly frustrated but genuinely loyal. Alternates between wanting to strangle him and appreciating him.
- **Barry G:** More skeptical than Kyle — "dude, why are we surrounding ourselves with this guy?"
- **Vance:** Immediately sees through the incompetence — "Vance can't litigate his way out of a paper bag"
- **Agent Chill:** Recognizes the threat immediately, unlike Kyle

## Abilities / Skills
- Can identify danger (but can't prevent it)
- Better strategic thinker than Kyle (low bar)
- Occasional moments of genuine leadership
- Good narrator — has a talent for summing up how fucked they are

## Weaknesses
- Can't say no to Kyle
- Despite seeing disasters coming, follows along anyway
- Gets dragged into every mess
- His pragmatism is useless against Kyle's chaos
