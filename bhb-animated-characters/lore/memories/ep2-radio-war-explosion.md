# Memory: The Radio Call, Keyboard Warriors, and the Explosion

**Episode:** 2
**Characters involved:** Kyle, Partner, Axel, Agent Chill, Keyboard Warriors
**Location:** Block City, Axel's Radio Station, Soul Block 420

## What Happened

### Agent Chill Arrives
An assassin named Agent Chill was dispatched to Block City (Soul Block 420) to deal with the hypnotism threat. His handler threatened to "cut off his eyelids" if he didn't report back. He considered the mission a public service — "taking down these goons."

### The Aftermath of the Mint
Kyle and the Partner surveyed the destroyed city. Kyle saw it as an improvement. The Partner noticed the NFTs had actually sold — "those slime balls actually did us a favor." Kyle suggested farming followers from the chaos.

### Axel's Radio Show
Kyle called into Axel's radio show to "make waves." Axel's show featured:
- A caller who lost $400,000 on Axel's advice (dismissed as "NFA, get butt punched")
- A caller who threatened to get his hands around Axel's "lizard neck"
- Kyle going on air and getting into a confrontation

Kyle was convinced this would help their brand. The Partner was skeptical.

### The Keyboard Warriors Rise
In response to Kyle's radio appearance, the Keyboard Warriors mobilized: "Brother Kyle, we have heard your plea over the radio and we too wish to be billionaires. Let the revolution begin!"

### The Explosion
A massive battle erupted. Agent Chill arrived to "ice" the BigHeads. The Keyboard Warriors clashed with opposing forces. An explosion rocked all of Block City.

## Key Dialogue
- Kyle: Compared his radio call to George Orwell ("that was a phone call" — it was a fictional radio broadcast about alien invasions)
- Kyle then went on an extremely disturbing tangent about... something involving shoving and screams
- Partner: "What the fuck are you talking about?"
- Kyle: "I mean let's go look, it's probably already the talk of the town"

## Consequences
- Block City devastated by the explosion
- BigHeads became both famous and hated
- Agent Chill caught in the crossfire
- Set up the need to flee Block City
