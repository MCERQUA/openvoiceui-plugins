# Memory: The Sellout, Utopia, and the Arrest

**Episode:** 3
**Characters involved:** Kyle, Partner, Barry G, Seth Green (cameo reference)
**Location:** Block City → Bus Stop → Utopia (via whale)

## What Happened

### The Sellout
After the explosion, the BHB mint had actually sold out. But the destruction meant they had to go on the run. Kyle: "Isn't that what founders do after selling out?" The Partner just wanted to get out of Block City.

### The Dumpster Incident
Before leaving, Kyle insisted on a detour. They encountered a female egg character ("I am clearly a female egg beats per Loopsy with boobies"). Kyle made the Partner throw things with him, ending up smelling like a dumpster. The Partner: "Why are we doing this?"

### Journey to Utopia
They took a city bus (Kyle had never taken one before). Kyle called ahead and got them on the list at Utopia — using Reggie the hypnotist AGAIN to secure their spot. They traveled to Utopia on a whale that someone lent them. Kyle: "I'm a born pilot."

### Utopia — The Cult
Utopia turned out to be a dystopian cult built around worshipping a mysterious "founder":
- A statue of the "glorious founder"
- A "top tier scientific facility" with stagnant experiments and classified prototypes
- Most residents on "extended break"
- A "historical gallery" containing: the founder's first words, a shoe the founder drank from, portraits by the founder of the founder, third grade report cards, dirty gym socks
- The Partner stumbled into the founder's shrine and was told "now that you've seen, you can never leave"

### The Escape and Arrest
They spotted someone who might have been Seth Green. Kyle grabbed him/something and they fled. The Partner thanked Kyle for getting him out. But they were immediately stopped by authorities: "We have reason to believe you're in possession of stolen property."

### Barry G Appears
Barry G — "the infamous garage sale con artist" — appeared and offered to help: "You boys want to get out of here? Stick with me, I got a plan."

## Key Character Moments
- Kyle using Reggie AGAIN despite everything: "I already put Reggie on it"
- Partner's rare moment of gratitude: "You're a good guy" — immediately followed by arrest
- The Utopia cult being a satire of worship-culture NFT projects

## Consequences
- Arrested for possession of stolen property + assault and battery
- Met Barry G, who became their guide through the legal/prison system
- Lost in the system with no way out
