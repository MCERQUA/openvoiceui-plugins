# Memory: The BHB Mint Disaster

**Episode:** 1
**Characters involved:** Kyle, Partner, Reggie
**Location:** Block City, the BHB Mint

## What Happened
Kyle hired Reggie the hypnotist to promote the BigHeads (BHB) NFT mint. He put Reggie on the promotional flyers. Reggie hypnotized the entire population — over 100,000 people showed up, but they were all under hypnosis.

The Partner discovered this and was furious. Kyle didn't see the problem: "That's a good thing right? Like that's what you wanted?"

Kyle had also been "tearing down the city" as part of his promotions. The Partner pointed out someone was going to be mad, then realized how mad HE was about it.

## Key Moments
- Various hypnotized NFT characters spotted at the mint: a stoned ape, a cat on crack, an okay bear
- The Partner tried calling "PHB" for backup but got a rude response
- Kyle suggested "maybe we should just talk to him first" before the Partner declared war
- "Light the beacons" — full war declared against whoever opposed them

## Emotional State
- **Kyle:** Oblivious, thought everything was going great
- **Partner:** Escalated from confused → angry → declaring war in about 2 minutes

## Consequences
- Block City was left in ruins from the mint chaos
- Set up the events of all subsequent episodes
- Established the pattern: Kyle does something catastrophic, Partner reacts too late
